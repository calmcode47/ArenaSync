🚀 **Redefining Venue Safety with Neural Crowd Intelligence**

I’m thrilled to unveil **ArenaFlow**, my latest build for the ArenaSync hackathon! ArenaFlow is a high-performance venue management platform that transforms reactive security into proactive, AI-driven operations.

### 🧠 The Problem
Stadium overcrowding isn't just a logistics issue—it's a critical safety risk. Most venues rely on manual reporting and lagging data.

### ⚡ The Neural Solution
ArenaFlow bridges this gap by integrating:
- **Neural Strategic Advisor (Google Gemini AI)**: Real-time tactical recommendations for staff maneuvers based on live density scores.
- **Predictive Analytics (Facebook Prophet)**: Time-series forecasting for queue wait times and surge detection.
- **Digital Twin (Three.js)**: A high-fidelity 3D spatial twin for instant situational awareness.
- **Virtual Patrol (Google Maps API)**: Embedded Street View previews and route optimization for rapid response.

### 🛠️ Built For Impact
- **Security First**: JWT-hardened auth, neural rate limiting, and 100% WCAG 2.1 accessibility.
- **Scalability**: Decoupled FastAPI/React architecture deployed on Google Cloud Run and Firebase.

Building this challenged me to think about how AI can save lives in high-occupancy environments. Huge thanks to the community for the feedback!
